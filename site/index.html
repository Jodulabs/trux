Why trux

Your machine. Your rules.

No cloud middleman, no per-seat markup, nothing exposed to the public internet.

Self-hosted

Runs as a systemd service on your own Linux box, working against your real checkout. Your code never leaves your hardware.

Your subscriptions

Drives the Claude, Codex, and opencode CLIs you're already logged into. Your plans, your keys — no extra billing in between.

Private by default

Reachable only over your tailnet via tailscale serve. The installer prints a URL and token — nothing public to lock down.

Setup

Up and running in one command.

01

Install

Run the one-line installer on your Linux box. It sets up the trux systemd service and a Node 22 runtime.

02

Connect

It prints your URL and access token, then configures tailscale serve so the box is reachable across your tailnet.

03

Drive

Open the URL on your phone, pick an agent, and start shipping — from the couch, the train, or anywhere.

Get started

Drive your agents from anywhere.
